---
title: ICD-10
description: How Obscyro maps SNOMED CT to ICD-10.
---

ICD-10 mappings come from SNOMED's official extended map refset. Each mapping carries a `mapGroup`, `mapPriority`, optional `mapAdvice`, and (when present) a `mapRule` that Obscyro parses into a structured `conditional` (`IFA` clause or raw expression).
