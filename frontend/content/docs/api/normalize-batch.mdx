<EndpointHeader method="POST" path="/v1/normalize-batch" />

Bulk variant of [`/v1/normalize`](/docs/api/normalize). Accepts up to 1,000 strings, runs them through the matcher with a concurrency of 20, and returns per-input matches plus aggregated `summaryStats`.

<Parameters title="Request body">
  <Param name="texts" type="string[]" required>
    1 to 1,000 phrases. Strings shorter than 2 characters after trimming get an empty <code>matches</code> array (no error).
  </Param>
</Parameters>
