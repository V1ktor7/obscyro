---
title: /v1/disambiguate
description: Pick the right SNOMED concept when the same surface text could mean several things, given clinical context.
---

<EndpointHeader method="POST" path="/v1/disambiguate" />

When `MI` could be myocardial infarction, mitral insufficiency, mental illness, or military intelligence, the right answer depends on what surrounds it. Send the ambiguous text plus a chunk of clinical context, and Obscyro scores each candidate by trigram similarity against its FSN and text definition.

<Parameters title="Request body">
  <Param name="text" type="string" required>
    The ambiguous phrase to resolve.
  </Param>
  <Param name="context" type="string" required>
    Surrounding clinical context (sentence, paragraph, or document). Used to score candidates.
  </Param>
  <Param name="candidateCodes" type="string[]">
    Optional pre-filtered candidate SCTIDs. When omitted, candidates are derived from <code>/v1/normalize</code>.
  </Param>
</Parameters>
